Privacy Policy — Music DNA

Last updated: 1 December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Music DNA (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Apple Music Library Data

To generate insights and visuals, we access your Apple Music library through Apple Music APIs. We may process and store:

  • Song, artist, album metadata
  • Play counts and listening statistics
  • Playlist structure

We do not access or store audio files.

Your library is processed via Firebase.

1.2 Account Information

If you sign in using Sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user identifier and (optionally) your name or email.

1.3 Generated Content

We may store insights, images, and animations generated for you so you can view your history and re-download content.

1.4 Analytics

We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage statistics, including:

  • App interactions
  • Feature usage
  • Device model, OS version, locale, app version

We also use Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting.

1.5 Technical Data

We may collect basic technical data such as device type, OS version, IP address, and performance information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Analyze your music library
  • Generate insights, images, and animations
  • Maintain your account and history
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure legal and security compliance

We do not sell your data.

3. Third-Party Services

Firebase

Used for authentication, storage, database, functions, and crash reporting.

Mixpanel

Used for anonymous analytics and app usage statistics.

OpenAI

Used to generate images, animations, or insights. Data is processed only for generation and is not used to train public models.

Apple Sign-In

Used for secure account authentication.

4. Data Retention

We retain your visuals, insights, and account data until you request deletion. Analytics and crash logs are kept as long as necessary for app improvement.
